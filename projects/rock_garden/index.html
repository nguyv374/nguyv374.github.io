Bubble Milk Tea Flavors

  • Classic Milk Tea

    This orginal classic flavor is made with black tea and milk with the classsical black tapioca pearls. The creamy and rich flavor is a popular among many and is a classic for a reason.


    Locations in NY

    *Can be found everywhere
    Trutea - 7 E 14th St
    Gong Cha - 27 St Marks Pl
    Kung Fu Tea - 31 Waverly Pl Unit 5

  • Taro Milk Tea

    This flavor is made from the Asian sweet root called taro. This purple drink offers a earthy, nutty, and vanilla flavor.


    Locations in NY

    Patea Buble Tea - 227 E 14th St
    Vivi Bubble Tea - 226 E 14th St
    Kung Fu Tea - 31 Waverly Pl Unit 5

  • Thai Milk Tea

    One of my favorites growing up is sthis sweet, creamy, orange milk tea has a silky texture and delious deep flavor. It is a strong black tea that is sweetened with sugar and condensed milk.


    Locations in NY

    Vivi Bubble Tea - 226 E 14th St
    CoCo Fresh Tea & Juice - 38 Lexington Ave
    Mi Tea - 101 MacDougal St

  • Fruit Tea

    From strawberry to honeydew, fruit teas have always been a popular choice to help cool you down. Not always made with milk but they always have many different varity of flavors.


    Locations in NY

    Trutea - 7 E 14th St
    Bambu - 38 E 23rd St
    Yaya Tea - 284 3rd Ave

  • Matcha Milk Tea

    An all time popular flavor, matcha is everywhere now. The classic green drink is made from matcha powder and is creamy and full of deep flavors.


    Locations in NY

    Koi - 32 Union Square E
    Bubbleology - 120 1/2 1st Ave
    Machi Machi - 33 W 32nd St

  • Brown Sugar Milk Tea

    A more newer flavor that has grown to popularity is brown sugar. It was created a few years ago by the famous Tiger Sugar in Taiwan. It consists of fresh milk tea and brown sugar syrup drizzled all over the sides.


    Locations in NY

    BIAO Sugar - 1271 Broadway
    Tiger Sugar - 197 Canal St
    Xing Fu Tang - 133 2nd Ave
